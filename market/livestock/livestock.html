Terms of Use

Last updated: May 2026 · Roots Livestock Module (Oroquieta City)

Welcome to Roots Livestock, part of the Roots platform for farmers and buyers in Oroquieta City, Misamis Occidental. By registering as a farmer, browsing listings, or using buy/cart features, you agree to these Terms.

1. Who may use this module

  • Buyers may browse, save listings, add items to cart, and arrange purchase (including Cash on Delivery) with sellers.
  • Farmers (sellers) must complete registration and, where required, Department of Agriculture (DA) verification before listing animals.
  • You must provide accurate information and use the service only for lawful livestock trade within Oroquieta and surrounding areas served by the platform.

2. Farmer registration

  • You agree that farm name, pickup address (barangay), contact details, and optional DA Farmer ID are truthful.
  • DA staff may review your application. A DA Verified badge is granted only after official approval—not automatically upon signup.
  • False identity, fake listings, or misuse of verification may lead to suspension (see Section 7).

3. Listings and content

  • Listings must describe real animals you own or are authorized to sell, trade, or offer for stud/service.
  • Photos, price, quantity, vaccination status, breed, age, and location must be substantially accurate.
  • You must not post stolen animals, endangered species without permits, misleading health claims, or illegal products.
  • Roots may remove or hide listings that violate these rules or receive valid reports.

4. Transactions and payments

  • Many sales use Cash on Delivery (COD) or meet-up payment agreed between buyer and seller.
  • Online payment (e.g. GCash/Maya) may be offered through a separate Payments module when available; Roots is not a bank and does not hold buyer funds unless that module explicitly states otherwise.
  • Disputes on price, delivery, animal health, or refunds are primarily between buyer and seller; DA or platform admins may assist in mediation where the full Roots system supports it.

5. Buyer and seller conduct

  • No harassment, spam, off-platform scams, or sharing others’ personal data without consent.
  • Do not circumvent verification to list as another person or from a suspended account.
  • Ratings and reviews must be honest and related to actual transactions.

6. Intellectual property

You keep ownership of photos and text you upload. You grant Roots a non-exclusive license to display that content on the platform for marketplace purposes. Do not upload images you do not have rights to use.

7. Suspension and termination

DA administrators or platform operators may suspend farmers, remove listings, or revoke verification for fraud, repeated complaints, or breach of these Terms. You may stop using the service at any time; local data in your browser may remain until you clear it.

8. Disclaimer

Roots Livestock is provided as a school/community project tool. Animal health, weight, and legal compliance (e.g. transport permits) remain your responsibility. The platform does not guarantee sale, purchase, or fitness of any animal.

9. Changes

We may update these Terms. Continued use after notice (in-app or via your group coordinator) means you accept the updated version.

10. Contact

Questions about these Terms: contact your Roots Livestock team lead or the DA Oroquieta City office supporting this module.

Data Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Complies with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) in spirit

This policy explains how the Roots Livestock module collects, uses, and protects personal information for users in Oroquieta City.

1. Personal data we collect

  • All users (browser/device): listings you view, cart/saved preferences stored locally, and technical logs if the server is enabled.
  • Farmers registering: farm/listing name, mobile phone number, pickup barangay and street/landmark, optional email, optional DA Farmer ID, and profile/listing photos you upload.
  • Buyers: cart and saved listings; order details when you proceed to payment (listing, quantity, seller name).
  • Transactions: order history, messages, and ratings when those features are connected to the database.

2. Why we use your data

  • To operate the marketplace (show listings, cart, checkout, notifications).
  • To verify farmers with DA Oroquieta and display trust badges.
  • To connect buyers and sellers for COD or future digital payments.
  • To improve safety, prevent fraud, and produce aggregated statistics (e.g. market prices) without identifying you in public reports.

3. Legal basis

We process data based on your consent (checkbox at registration), contract (providing the service you asked for), and legitimate interest (security, fraud prevention, DA regulatory support for farmers).

4. Who we share data with

  • Other users: buyers see seller name, location area, and listing details; sellers see buyer contact only when needed for an order (as implemented in Payments/orders).
  • DA administrators: registration and verification data for farmer approval, suspension, and audits.
  • Platform operators / developers: STEM project team and hosting provider, under confidentiality for system maintenance.
  • We do not sell your personal data to advertisers.

5. Storage and security

  • Data may be stored in the app database (MySQL) and temporarily in your browser (localStorage) for cart, saved items, and drafts.
  • We use reasonable technical measures (access control, HTTPS when deployed on a proper server). No system is 100% secure—avoid sharing passwords on shared devices.

6. How long we keep data

Account and listing data are kept while your account is active and as required for DA records, disputes, or law. You may request deletion subject to legal retention rules.

7. Your rights (RA 10173)

You may request access, correction, erasure, or objection to certain processing by contacting the DA office or Roots team. We will respond within a reasonable time required by law.

8. Children

This service is intended for adults (18+) or minors with parent/guardian consent for farm activities. We do not knowingly collect data from young children without consent.

9. Links to other modules

Roots includes separate modules (Videos, Payments, Registration, etc.). Each may have its own policy when you leave Livestock.

10. Updates

We may revise this policy. Material changes will be shown in-app; continued use after update means you have read the new version.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: DA Oroquieta City (Livestock program) or your Roots Livestock module coordinator.
